## Selection Timeline

| Phase | Timeline | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Recruitment | Days 1-7 | Post job, collect applications |
| Screening | Days 7-10 | Review top 10 applicants |

- **Monthly Stipend**: 500-1,000 ETHIC/month (based on hours)
