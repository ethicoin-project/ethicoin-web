# Phase II Community Engagement Activities

## Daily Activities

### Morning (9 AM UTC)
- Post announcement/news on Telegram
- Tweet market update or news
- Reply to @mentions (within 1 hour)
- Check moderation queue

### Afternoon (3 PM UTC)
- Post community discussion topic
- Tweet engagement content (poll, question)
- Like & RT 5-10 community posts
- Respond to support tickets

### Evening (9 PM UTC)
- Post CTA or highlight
- Tweet fun/engagement content
- Thank new followers (personalized)
- Archive old messages
