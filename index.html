⚖️ THE ETHICAL FRAMEWORK

ETHIC is built on the principle that your digital identity is your private property. In the current AI era, your data is used to train trillion-dollar models without your consent or compensation.

Our protocol ensures:

  • Consent-First: Your data is never accessed without explicit on-chain permission.
  • Direct Rewards: Value flows directly to the data provider, not intermediaries.
  • Agnostic Privacy: Zero-knowledge proofs protect your identity while verifying your data value.
