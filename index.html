Protocol Features
🧠
Data Sovereignty

Users register their data hash on-chain and grant access only with explicit consent. The DataSovereignty smart contract enforces this cryptographically — no party can access data without a recorded on-chain permission.

DataSovereignty · Sepolia ↗

Ethical AI

Every AI training event is logged on-chain via the AITrainingLog contract — who used which data, when, and for what purpose. Immutable, public, verifiable by anyone on Sepolia Etherscan.
